Adding new nodes

New nodes can be added by running the following command on a node:

your-device # bb link {{ device.data.security_code }}

This however requires the BigBoards fabric to be available on the node. The firmware comes pre-installed on BigBoards Hexes. In the future the fabric will also be made available for other platforms.
